Grateful Place

Hello!

Sharing gratitude is such a simple, easy way for you to improve your life. Research shows that cultivating gratitude is a great way to become happier and remain happier — as opposed to events like getting a new car or a raise which only temporarily improve your mood. And it only takes a couple minutes every day!

Personally, I was motivated to create this site after beginning a "daily gratitude" practice, where I'd think of something I was grateful for every morning. I started doing this after reading that it was an easy, powerful way to step off the "happiness treadmill", the constant striving for more things and accomplishments.

After a short time, I noticed that I did in fact feel happier. Then I tried something new: sharing my gratitude on Facebook and Twitter. In doing so I learned that sharing my gratitude and seeing others' positive responses made me feel even happier.

If this sounds like something you'd like to try, please join!

Sincerely,
Daniel Higginbotham
